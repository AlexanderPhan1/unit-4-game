Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game; this is your Target Number.

There are four crystals below; clicking on one of them will add a certain amount to your Score Total.

The objective of the game is to match your own Score to the random Target number. Match the number and you win. Exceed the target's amount and you will lose the game.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it, and with each new game the value of the crystals changes.
